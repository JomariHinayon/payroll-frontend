What is the Barangay Mobile application?
The Barangay Mobile Application is dedicated for the employee of the barangay. The mobile app can help the employee to monitor their time in and time out, and also their attendance. Through the app they can receive notification if they can claim their salary. Lastly, the employee can send a request to Leave through the mobile app.
